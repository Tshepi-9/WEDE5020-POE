About Us

Business History: Scarlet Raimsy Clothing’s was Established in 2019 after a survey about women’s clothing that was conducted in august in honour of women’s month. Scarlet Raimsy Clothing’s focuses on empowering women to express individuality by customizing outfits to their body type, preferences, and occasions. Our team believes every woman deserves to wear clothing that fits her body and personality perfectly.

Mission

To create quality clothes that will make women comfortable and happy, and custom-fit clothing that celebrates every woman’s unique style.
