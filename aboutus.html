About Us

Business History: Scarlet Raimsy Clothing was established in 2019 after a survey about women’s clothing conducted in August in honour of Women’s Month. We focus on empowering women to express individuality by customizing outfits to their body type, preferences, and occasions.

Mission

To create quality clothes that make women comfortable and happy, and custom-fit clothing that celebrates every woman’s unique style.

Founder / CEO

The founder of this business is Scarlet Buthelezi.

Personal Message: YOU DESERVE TO SHINE, MY DARLINGS! Feel comfortable in your skin… Love is amongst us women.

Store Details

Our boutique is located in Johannesburg, South Africa. We offer in-store consultations and an online ordering platform for your convenience.
